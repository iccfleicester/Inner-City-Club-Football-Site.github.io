About Us

A Football and Community organisation based in the heart of Leicester, Inner City Club Football (ICCF) has been successfully serving the community since the summer of 2003.
Creating football opportunities for young people living in Highfields and neighbouring areas, ICCF, aims to unify diversity across Leicester.
ICCF is committed in its endeavour to provide quality coaching and education to build up 5-16-year-olds, with integrity and the ability to play football at a competitive level.
Encouraging a modern, child-friendly approach to youth football, ICCF dedicates a challenging and learning mentality for children and young teens. Working together with a proactive attitude, Coaches at ICCF foster a learning environment for children that put their development needs at the centre.
ICCF has taken part in a range of matches and competitions nationwide.
